FAQ

What you should know before starting.

Can MPU be guaranteed?

No. Serious preparation cannot promise a guarantee. It can help you avoid typical mistakes and explain real change.

Is online preparation enough?

Often yes, if it is structured and fits your file. Complex cases need personal support.

When do I need abstinence proof?

That depends on reason, history and file. Alcohol, drugs and cannabis should be checked early.
