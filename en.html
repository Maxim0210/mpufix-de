Typical MPU cases

We start where your real problem is.

Every MPU case is different. The first step is to understand why the authority requested the MPU and what the assessor will expect from you.

Alcohol

Drinking pattern, change of behaviour, risk situations and possible abstinence proof need a clear structure.

Drugs or cannabis

Last use, screenings, abstinence, personal change and future rules must fit your file.

Points or offences

The MPU interview checks insight, responsibility and realistic rules for your future driving behaviour.
